Theodore is confused as he enters into a new world. He glances around at his surroundings as he is trying to figure out what to make of this new place he is in.

Theodore stumbles upon a group of life sized toys. They tower over him as he cautiously studies them.

He finds a group of signs directing visitors to all of the different attractions and lands.

Theodore gets lost and finds himself in a cemetary.

He wanders amongst the gravestones trying to find his way out.

Through a series of unpredictable events, Theodore discovers that this entire world is all seen through a camera lens! He is inside the world of film.

Having made some sense of the world he found himself in, Theodore decides to explore a bit more.

He helps himself to some goodies in an enchanted bakery.

He climbs to the top of a tall tower.

He joins a group of pirates on a swashbuckling adventure.

He makes friends and finds all types of people and creatures within this new world.

Theodore is feeling on top of the world. Adventure is at his fingertips.
